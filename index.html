ABOUT
ROCKO'S CHICKEN

Established in 2017 headed by Mr. Joven P. Dy,
a veteran in chicken industry, with 4 skilled employees.

The company was named Agros Trofi Corporation that is currently situated in Marilao, Bulacan. The Rocko’s chicken is made of different herbs and spices from different parts of the world and it has a heavenly taste that will make you want for more.

Agros Trofi Corporation had a humble beginning, it only started with 3 following stores, San Jose del Monte, Marilao and Meycauayan, Bulacan. The company is standing still with a full hope of success in the near future.

As time passed by, Rocko’s is starting to create its own name in the market, People are starting to look for Rocko’s Chicken to satisfy their cravings. It became popular not only because of the taste of chicken but also the sauce that has a mouthwatering taste that you can’t resist.

Rockos Herb-Roasted Chicken gets bigger and bigger in market presence by opening stores in Region III and in National Capital Region and also offers franchising. Through the hardships and camaraderie of all the people behind this company Rockos Herb-Roasted Chicken achieved its desired goal in 2018 by ending the year with 63 stores. 

Nowadays, Rocko’s chicken is genuinely accepted in the market resulting to a satisfying feedback and success to the company.
